Lasagna

the world's best lasagna

Who doesn't love lasagna? It is one of the best pasta dishes in the world and is great for parties or just for the family

This is our top choice for a lasagna recipe. It is quick and easy and has an amazing taste. It only take about an hour to prepare and two hours to cook

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic,crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5) ounce cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley,divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese,sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
